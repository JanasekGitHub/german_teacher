      How to Use German Reader
      
        
      
      
      
      
        
      Reading
      
        
        
          
      • Upload a German PDF or .txt file from the Library.
        • 
          
      • Click a document to open it in the reader.
        • 
          
      • Use A− / A+ to adjust font size.
        • 
          
      • Your reading position is saved automatically.
        • 
        
      

        
      Marking Vocabulary
      
        
        
          
      • Double-click a word to mark it as unknown vocabulary (yellow highlight).
        • 
        
      

        
      Marking Grammar
      
        
        
          
      • Select a phrase or sentence by dragging, then right-click to mark it as a grammar structure (blue highlight).
        • 
        
      

        
      Removing Annotations
      
        
        
          
      • Right-click on any highlighted word or phrase to remove it.
        • 
        
      

        
      Exporting Prompts
      
        
        
          
      • Click Export to generate AI prompts for your vocabulary and grammar.
        • 
          
      • Copy the prompt and paste it into Gemini (or any AI) to study.
        • 
          
      • The vocabulary prompt teaches you words with meaning, examples, and mnemonics.
        • 
          
      • The grammar prompt explains the structure and gives you translation exercises.
        • 
        
      

        
      Settings
      
        
        
          
      • Click Settings to customize the AI prompt templates.
        • 
          
      • Use {items} as a placeholder for your annotated items.
